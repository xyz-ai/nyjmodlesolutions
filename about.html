About Us

Professional Mold & Plastic Solution Provider

Taizhou Kailong Mold & Plastic Co., Ltd. was established in Huangyan, Taizhou — known as “China Mold Town”. With 15 years of rich experience in mold design, manufacturing and plastic injection production, we have become a trusted partner for customers from more than 30 countries.

We specialize in used mold trading, custom new mold development and high-quality plastic product injection molding. Whether you need to buy second-hand molds to reduce costs or need us to design and produce brand new molds from scratch, we can provide one-stop professional services.
