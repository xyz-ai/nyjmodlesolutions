About Us

Professional Mold & Plastic Solution Provider

Taizhou Kailong Mold & Plastic Co., Ltd. is located in Huangyan, Taizhou, known as “China Mold Town”. With over 15 years of experience in mold design, manufacturing and plastic injection production, we serve customers from more than 30 countries worldwide.

In addition to used mold trading, we have strong capabilities in automotive molds and electronic & precision molds. We support custom mold development and plastic injection production based on customer drawings and technical requirements.


Why Choose Kailong?

15 years focus on mold and injection molding · Complete process from design → manufacturing → injection → inspection · Fast response and delivery · Rich experience in used mold evaluation and renovation · Stable export quality.

Major Mold Application Fields

  • Household molds for daily-use plastic products
  • Automotive molds for interior and exterior components
  • Electronic & precision molds with tight tolerance

Our Solutions

  • Custom mold development (household / automotive / electronic)
  • Plastic injection production (80T–1600T)
