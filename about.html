About Us

Professional Mold & Plastic Solution Provider

Taizhou Kailong Mold & Plastic Co., Ltd. is located in Huangyan, Taizhou, known as “China Mold Town”. With over 15 years of experience in mold design, manufacturing and plastic injection production, we serve customers from more than 30 countries worldwide.

In addition to used mold trading, we have strong capabilities in automotive molds and electronic & precision molds. Our team supports custom mold development and plastic injection production for household, automotive and electronic applications.


Why Choose Kailong?

15 years focus on mold and injection molding · Complete process from design → manufacturing → injection → inspection · Fast response and delivery · Rich experience in used mold evaluation and renovation · Stable export quality for Europe, America, Middle East and Southeast Asia markets.

Major Mold Application Fields

  • Household Molds – Storage boxes, buckets, chairs, tables and daily-use plastic products.
  • Automotive Molds – Interior and exterior plastic components and functional automotive parts.
  • Electronic & Precision Molds – Electronic housings and high-tolerance plastic components.

Our Solutions

  • Used Mold Trading – Professional evaluation, renovation and supply of used molds to reduce customer investment cost.
  • Custom Mold Development – Full process support from product drawing to mold manufacturing for household, automotive and electronic projects.
  • Plastic Injection Production – 30+ injection machines (80T–1600T) supporting trial and mass production.
  • Mold Maintenance & Repair – Mold modification, maintenance and spare parts processing to extend mold life.

Our Expertise

  • Mold design and manufacturing for complex structures
  • Automotive and precision mold engineering experience
  • Stable injection molding quality control
  • Export-oriented project communication and delivery
