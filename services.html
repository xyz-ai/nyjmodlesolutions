Our Products

We specialize in supplying used injection molds for a wide range of household plastic products, including storage boxes, buckets, chairs, tables, cups, basins and other daily-use items.

All used molds are carefully inspected. Trial molding and minor modification services are available before delivery.

Note: In addition to the used molds shown on this page, we also manufacture custom automotive molds and electronic & precision molds based on customer drawings and technical requirements.
